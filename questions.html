How early should i get to the airport?

You should arrive preferably at least 2 hours before a international flight and 1 hour before a domestic flight.

What is the maximum luggage weight?

No more than 50 pounds (23kg) for international flights and 28 pounds (13kg) for domestic flights .

How do i contact you if i want to cancel or change my trip?

Can I cancel my trip?

Yes! Send us a message with your details and we will proccess your application as fast as possible. Keep in mind u will still have to pay the fee.
